3s & LMC-KAISA OUTREACH PROGRAM

Peso para sa Preso

The Peso para sa Preso Gift giving Outreach program brings joy and hope to inmates through thoughtful gifts during festive seasons and special occasions. Supported by donors and volunteers, it provides essential items, books, and educational materials to uplift spirits and foster connections among incarcerated individuals.

By spreading positivity and goodwill, the program contributes to their rehabilitation and emotional well-being, showcasing the power of collective kindness in creating a more inclusive society, regardless of their circumstances.

FDTP & LMC-KAISA GIFT GIVING

Tablet Donation Batch 1

Tablet Donation Batch 1 initiates a transformative effort to bridge the digital gap and improve access to education and communication for underserved communities. Through collaboration and FDTP, this program provides essential tablets, empowering recipients with tools for learning, connectivity, and growth.

By equipping individuals with these resources, it not only brings immediate benefits but also sets the stage for long-term empowerment and socio-economic progress, embodying community solidarity and innovation to create positive change and opportunities for those in need.
